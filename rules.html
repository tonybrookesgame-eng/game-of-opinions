How It Works

Game of Opinions is a football management game built on the belief that pure football knowledge should trump spreadsheets and template teams.

The Golden Rule: You can only select ONE player from any single Premier League club. Every team is a puzzle.

1. Building Your XI

Squad Constraints

  • One Player Per Club: You must select 11 players from 11 different clubs.
  • Goalkeeper Flexibility: To mitigate the volatility of selection headaches, Goalkeeper picks are made at the **Club level**. Your team earns points for the position’s performance regardless of which individual starts, keeping your defensive strategy agnostic to late squad rotations or injury withdrawals.
  • No Budget: There are no player prices. If you want Haaland, you can have him—but you lose access to every other Man City player.
  • Formations: You can choose from standard formations (4-4-2, 4-3-3, 3-5-2, 3-4-3, 4-5-1).

2. Weekly Match Predictions

Every week, your knowledge is tested on specific match outcomes alongside your player selections.

Weekly Picks

  • Club to Score First: Pick the club you think will open the scoring across the weekend fixtures. 10 PTS
  • Minute of First Goal: Predict the exact minute the first goal will be scored in the featured match. 40 PTS

The Bet Builder (Risk & Reward)

This is where leagues are won or lost. Every week, a "Scout's Bet" is presented. You must decide if it will land (YES) or fail (NO).

  • The Sacrifice: It costs 5 Points to enter a prediction.
  • YES: High Reward / High Risk. If the bet lands, you win massive points. If it fails, you lose points.
  • NO: Stability / Low Risk. If the bet fails, you win small points. If it lands, you lose massive points.

3. Season Predictions

Long-term vision pays off. These picks are locked at the start of the season (or when you join) and provide a huge point injection at the end of the campaign.

  • Premier League Winner 25 PTS
  • FA Cup Winner 50 PTS
  • Top Goalscorer 25 PTS
  • Most Red Cards (Team) 75 PTS

4. Scoring System

Points are awarded based on real-world performance. (Specific player point values to be finalized).

Standard Scoring Categories:

  • All Players: Appearance, Goals, Assists, Yellow/Red Cards.
  • Defenders/Keepers: Clean Sheets, Goals Conceded.
  • Opinion Multiplier: Points are boosted if you select a "Differential" player (owned by less than 10% of the league).

5. AI Scout Integration

Use the "Opinion AI" icons on the Season and Team pages to get insights from legends like R9 or Fergie. Their analysis is based on current form and historical data, but remember—it's just an opinion.

6. Deadlines & Changes

  • XI Changes: You can change your team as much as you like until the first kickoff of the Gameweek.
  • Weekly Picks: Locked at the same time as your team.
  • Season Picks: Locked permanently once submitted.
